Wave Music are to Wave Studios as Fergie is to the Black Eyed Peas - they were great without us, but with the new addition, they’re even greater.

Having now been in existence for (almost!) a year, but with 25 years of experience, Wave Music have already put music to campaigns for brands such as Nike, P&O, Fiat, VW, Legoland, Volvo, Dell, Renault, Sky Sports and others. We’ve repped the voice of sync, musicians and creativity at BIME festival in Bilbao, and we’ve thrown parties and even gigs to celebrate the awesomeness of music. Artists such as SOAK, Lisbon Kid, and even humble ol’ Grandmaster Flash (!) have graced our stage.

We love our jobs and we’re not afraid to show it. Our days are spent listening to music, speaking to awesome people and surrounding ourselves in and around a company where there is [[..so.much..]] creativity every single day that it’s impossible for it not to sink in. We’re really, really lucky and we bloody well know it.

Whether you need bespoke composition, licensing & negotiation of existing tracks, creative music searches or even just a friend, we will be here for you.

Music
Composition

We create great music for the advertising, digital, television and film industries. We have a roster of 30 (and growing) artists, composers, orchestrators, arrangers and producers all of whom who are established in their own right and working in the music industry today. Our representation of established artists and real musicians is what sets our work apart and keeps our clients coming back for more.

Finding the right piece of music can be an impossible task and the licensing process a minefield, if you don't know what you are doing of course. Our team of supervisors are completely independent of labels and publishers allowing them complete creative freedom on every project. Our team are experienced in finding the best tracks and are tough negotiators which always results in the best and most cost effective answer to any brief.

Adapting existing pieces of music for a commercial, programme or film can add an extra sense of originality whilst retaining familiarity. Our re-records have won countless industry awards including the coveted D&AD Yellow Pencils, British Arrows and Andy Awards to name a few. Our Creative Director Nick Payne is often called upon to assist publishers and labels with the re-recording of their own catalogue for sync purposes.

We are fully equipped to take care of all the necessary legal issues and negotiations that occur during the process of creating and sourcing music for our clients. We also have a musicologist who is always on hand to advise on copyright issues throughout a production.

We work closely with our clients to develop the right sound for their brand and align that brand with the perfect composition, song, artist or combination of all of these vital elements. From simply finding the right piece of music for a commercial, to facilitating deals between artists and brands we can assist with the production of a campaign from early script and concept stages right through to final production.
